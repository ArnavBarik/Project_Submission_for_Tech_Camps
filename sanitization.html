About Pandemics and Being Safe



A pandemic is announced official when a disease or virus is spread worldwide in a largegovernments of the world put their scale. For eg- Covid-19, The Plague,Spanish Flu, Ebola etc. So, to keep everybody safe, all the governments of the world put their countries into lockdown andeveryone is expected to follow all the safety protocals and precautions. For a respiratory virus like Covid-19, everyone should wear a mask, socially distance, stay at home and not go to crowded places so as to not get infected by the disease or virus.
